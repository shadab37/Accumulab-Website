The biggest issue for labs is the irreproducibility of data.

This issue alone costs the Life Science industry $3 Billion per year.
Accumulab is a platform geared to streamline and simplify all of these processes by creating an
end-to-end ecosystem that will provide access at the lowest level of data from the researchers themselves to
the highest level maintained by administrators.

Designed with you in mind

Accumulab is aimed for researchers of all levels, and for research groups/institutions of all sizes.

Passive Collection

Accumulab scrapes the web to bring you personalized data to benefit your research and plan future moves.

Active Collaboration

Expand your network. Easily reach out to like-minded researchers and take part in thought-capital discussions.

Other players in the space have identified a gap in the market around tackling the complexity and pace of research, thus
they have built solutions specific to certain needs. But a larger hole exists around this top to bottom connectivity that
would enable all of these other solutions to work more resourcefully and have a larger impact for each institution. That
is why we work with universities and research institutes, as the Accumulab platform would enable them to remove the
hurdles that exist thus far that prevent such entities from delivering value at the pace they should be.

  • Live Feed

    See everything going on in your laboratory in real-time. All notifications aggregated into one place.

  • Automation

    Easily generate pre-filed documentation, so you can focus on what's new.

  • Administration

    A visual dashboard tracking work being done, complete with inventory tracking and communications options.

  • Research Network

    Discover your institution's knowledge base. Search through several means of data streams to find what you need. Based on your data, we recommend several results of interest.

  • Back Office

    As an administrator, you can easily oversee all the projects going on in your institution.

  • Analytics

    With Accumulab serving as the bridge for all research data being collected in your organization, we help you make decisions on budgeting and tracking ROI proficiently and intelligently.
